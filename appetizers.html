Appetizer Recipes

Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Toast baguette slices until golden brown.
  2. Mix tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Spoon mixture onto toasted bread and serve immediately.

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 12 large button mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Remove stems from mushrooms and chop them finely.
  3. Mix chopped stems with cream cheese, Parmesan, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  4. Fill mushroom caps with mixture and bake for 20 minutes.
