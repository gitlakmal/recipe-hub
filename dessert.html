Chocolate Mousse

Silky, airy, and deeply chocolatey ‚Äî a classic dessert that feels fancy but is easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 200 g dark chocolate (60‚Äì70%), chopped
  • 3 large eggs, separated (use pasteurized eggs if preferred)
  • 1/4 cup (50 g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (240 ml) heavy cream, cold
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Melt chocolate gently over a double boiler or in short microwave bursts; let cool slightly.
  2. Whip cold cream with vanilla to soft peaks; refrigerate.
  3. Beat egg whites with a pinch of salt to soft peaks, then gradually add sugar and beat to glossy medium peaks.
  4. Stir egg yolks into the cooled melted chocolate until smooth.
  5. Fold one-third of the whipped cream into the chocolate to lighten, then fold in the remaining cream.
  6. Gently fold in the meringue until just combined. Spoon into cups and chill 2‚Äì3 hours before serving.
Pro Tip: Add a pinch of espresso powder to intensify the chocolate flavor. Top with shaved chocolate or berries.

No-Bake Strawberry Cheesecake Parfaits

Layered, creamy, and fruity desserts that come together quickly ‚Äî no oven needed.

Ingredients:

  • 200 g digestive biscuits or graham crackers, crushed
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 225 g cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) heavy cream, cold
  • 1/3 cup (70 g) powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1.5 cups fresh strawberries, diced (plus extra for garnish)
  • 1‚Äì2 tsp lemon juice (optional)

Steps:

  1. Combine crushed biscuits with melted butter until the texture resembles wet sand.
  2. Beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Whip cold cream to soft peaks and fold into the cream cheese mixture.
  4. Toss diced strawberries with a little lemon juice if desired.
  5. Layer in glasses: biscuit crumbs, cheesecake cream, strawberries; repeat layers.
  6. Chill for at least 1 hour. Garnish with extra berries before serving.
Pro Tip: Swap strawberries for mango, blueberries, or a quick berry compote. Add a drizzle of strawberry jam for extra flavor.
